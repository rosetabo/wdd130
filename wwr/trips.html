Gentle Rapids Adventure

Perfect for families and first-timers, enjoy a calm but exciting ride through scenic landscapes. Half-day trip with a picnic lunch included. Our most popular trip for those new to rafting.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Minimum age: 8 years
  • Price: $50 per person

Intermediate Thrill Run

Take on bigger waves and faster currents! This trip is great for those looking for more excitement while still feeling safe. Includes class III rapids and a riverside lunch.

  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Minimum age: 12 years
  • Price: $90 per person
Book Now

Extreme White Water Challenge

For experienced rafters only! This trip offers adrenaline-pumping rapids and non-stop action from start to finish. Tackle class IV-V rapids with our most experienced guides.

  • Duration: Full day
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Price: $150 per person
