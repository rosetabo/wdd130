About Whitewater Adventures

Discover our passion for adventure and commitment to your safety and enjoyment

Our History

Founded in 2005 by river enthusiasts Mark and Sarah Johnson, Whitewater Adventures began with a single raft and a dream to share the thrill of river running with others. What started as weekend excursions for friends quickly grew into a full-fledged adventure company.

Our first commercial trip had just 6 participants, but word spread quickly about the incredible experiences we provided. By 2010, we were operating on three different rivers with a fleet of 12 rafts.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most respected rafting companies in the region, with over 50 trained guides and safety personnel. We've introduced thousands of people to the joy of whitewater rafting while maintaining an impeccable safety record.

We continue to innovate with new trip offerings and maintain our commitment to environmental stewardship of the rivers we love.

Adventure Awaits You!

Whitewater Adventures offers thrilling rafting experiences for all skill levels. Our expert guides ensure your safety while you enjoy the adventure of a lifetime.
